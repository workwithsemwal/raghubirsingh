Raghubir Singh Karlupia (Sociologist)

Raghubir Singh Karlupia

I am an accomplished sociologist with a passion for consultancy and capacity building in the areas of Gender Equality, Social Safeguard, Livelihood, Community Mobilization, Behaviour Change Communication (BCC) and Information Education & Communication (IEC), with over 15 years of experience in these fields, I have established myself as an expert in the development sector. My expertise in Social Safeguard, Capacity Building and Livelihood has been used to assist various not-for-profit and public organizations in developing and executing various projects. I have also used my knowledge in BCC and IEC to design custom communication campaigns that are effective and engaging. I have a post-graduation in Sociology and have attended various national and regional workshops on social development. I have a deep understanding of the various aspects of community development and have successfully helped numerous organizations in their social development projects. I am an excellent team player and have an eye for detail. I am also an effective communicator and have the ability to understand the needs of different stakeholders. I am passionate about making a positive and lasting impact in the communities. Overall, I am a highly experienced and competent sociologist, who brings with the wealth of knowledge and experience.
